Kabam

Repositioning a mobile gaming leader

After expanding from a small gaming studio founded in 2009 into an international, multi-platform game publisher, Kabam needed a new corporate site that would reflect their maturity and continue to grow with them.

  • Responsive Design
  • Content Strategy

  • Marketing & Branding

Evolving the Kabam brand

Kabam came to Brick Design for a responsive site that would represent them well to investors, third-party studios, prospective employees, and the news media.

First impressions

We created a corporate home that highlighted the company’s progress, goals, media coverage, job openings, and partnership opportunities. We also evolved their brand language to compete with established gaming companies like EA and Chillingo.

Company story

The company section covers how Kabam is reshaping the industry, showcasing their achievements, partners, and current investors. We also included a listing of top in-house and third-party games to give a record of their continued success.

Media

We focused on giving the corporate communications team the best possible tools to maintain Kabam’s press relations. A custom Django admin simplifies publishing press kits, news coverage, and employees’ insights into the industry.

Careers

As Kabam continued to open international offices, they needed to promote the unique perks each location had to offer. We added office pages to show off the diverse work environments and staff personalities, and included the same search and filtering by department present on the all jobs page.

Kabam’s HR department wanted the Careers page to convey the Company’s belief in both work-life balance and their commitment to quality and professionalism. We achieved this by including Day in the Life interviews with employees from a variety of departments and positions.
