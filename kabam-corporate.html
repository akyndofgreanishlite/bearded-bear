Kabam

Repositioning a mobile gaming leader

Kabam started as a single gaming studio in 2008. After growing to become a multi-platform game publisher with international offices, its corporate site needed to be updated to reflect this and have room to expand with the company.

Evolving the Kabam brand

Kabam came to Brick Design looking for a responsive, multi-faceted corporate site that could speak equally well to investors, third-party studios, prospective employees, and the news media.

First impressions

We created a corporate home that highlights the company’s progress and ambitions, media coverage, job openings, and ways to partner with Kabam. We also evolved their brand language, putting them on even ground with established gaming companies like EA and Zynga.

Company story

The company section covers how Kabam is reshaping the industry, showcasing their achievements, partners, and current investors. We also included a listing of top in-house and third-party games to give a record of their continued success.

Media

We focused on giving the corporate communications team the best possible tools to maintain Kabam’s press relations. A custom Django admin simplifies publishing press kits, news coverage, and employees’ insights into the industry.

Careers

As Kabam continues to add international offices they needed to promote unique perks each had to offer. That meant adding location specific job pages in addition to comprehensive search and filtering by department.

Human resources at Kabam wanted to emphasize the balance between work and play at Kabam without compromising commitment to quality and professionalism, and Day in the Life interviews do just that by giving insights from a variety departments and positions.
