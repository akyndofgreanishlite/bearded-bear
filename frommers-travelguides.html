Frommers’ Travel Guides

Weather Icons

When translating a selection of seven travel guides from the Frommer’s Day by Day series to be published digitally, Inkling wanted to evolve these titles beyond static references. To begin to make them real-time references, we provided travelers with real-time location-based information.

  • Icon Design

We started by introducing weather forecasts into chapter openings powered by Weather Underground. Read more about the Inkling editions on the Frommers’ store, or read on Inkling for Web.
