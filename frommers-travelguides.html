Frommers’ Travel Guides

Weather Icons

When translating a selection of 7 travel guides from the Frommer’s Day-by-Day series to be published digitally, Inkling wanted to begin to make these titles truly dynamic, and able to provide travelers with location-based information. We started by introducing weather forecasts powered by the Weather Underground api into the chapter openings.

Read more about the Inkling editions on the Frommers' store, or view live in Inkling for Web.
