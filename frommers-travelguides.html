Frommers’ Travel Guides

Weather Icons

When translating a selection of seven travel guides from the Frommer’s Day-by-Day series to be published digitally, Inkling wanted to begin to make these titles truly dynamic, and able to provide travelers with location-based information.

We started by introducing weather forecasts into the chapter openings powered by the Weather Underground api.

Read more about the Inkling editions on the Frommers' store, or read on Inkling for Web.
