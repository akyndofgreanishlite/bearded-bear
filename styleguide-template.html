A web-based experimental branding piece, including aspects of both documentary and interactive experience, to show upper level professors and seniors in a different light, being more than just authoritarian figures, and as mentors and resources for first year students to come to for advice. Aimed at building a better sense of community within the college as a result, beginning with the foundation year students.

The sequence begins with an introductory video, previewing the short response format used later, and explaining the purpose of this interactive piece. A questionnaire is next to determine a student's aptitudes, along with accompanying results and detailed breakdown. Following that a student is shown a series of interviews* on four main topics, current design trends, past personal experiences, and interdisciplinary work and experiences in the given design concentration. Lastly, a global navigation is displayed, to allow students to explore the video responses to all the topic areas.
