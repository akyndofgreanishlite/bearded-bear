Vobok

Push-to-talk messaging

The Apple app store was saturated voice messaging apps, but they weren’t both functional and beautiful. Vobok came to Brick Design to take advantage of this deficit and realize their dream of easy, convenient voice messaging as an alternative to driving and texting.

  • Mobile App Design
  • User Experience

Sharing your voice

Voice messaging as personal status updates didn’t turn out to be as lucrative as they’d hoped, compared to celebrities, comedians, and public figures to broadcast to fans numbering in the millions. This made Vobok shift focus to enable a radio-like feed of voice messages, able to be paired with photos and text to encourage spontaneity.
